Plot

The movie follows Joey Orange, a charismatic and eccentric character who becomes obsessed with the sensation of pulp in his drinks. One day, he discovers a mysterious juice bar called "Pulp Paradise," known for its unique and addictive pulpy concoctions.
Joey soon finds himself entangled in a pulp underworld filled with eccentric juice enthusiasts, rival juice bars, and a black market for rare and exotic pulps. As he navigates this juicy adventure, Joey encounters bizarre characters like Vincent Squeezetino, a hitman who always carries a citrus reamer, and Mia Smoothie, a dance-floor diva with a passion for blended beverages.
The narrative weaves through interconnected stories involving pulp heists, juice mixology competitions, and a quest for the ultimate, rarest pulp that promises an otherworldly experience. All the while, the characters grapple with their own "pulp addictions" and the consequences of their juiciest decisions.
