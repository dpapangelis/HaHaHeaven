Plot

In the small town of Humorville, a series of comically exaggerated murders begin to take place, each mimicking iconic scenes from classic horror movies. The killer wears a ridiculous mask shaped like an exclamation mark, and the town is thrown into a state of panic. The residents, however, are surprisingly unfazed, as they believe it's just another elaborate prank.
As the body count rises, a group of friends led by the witty and self-aware protagonist, Max Jester, starts to investigate the murders. Max quickly realizes that the killer is targeting people who have unwittingly become stereotypes of horror movie characters. The group, consisting of a horror movie buff, a sarcastic cheerleader, a tech-savvy nerd, and a stoner with an uncanny ability to survive ridiculous situations, decides to outwit the killer and save their town.
Throughout the film, the characters break the fourth wall, referencing and parodying famous horror films, while the killer continues to strike in increasingly absurd and comedic ways. The climax reveals the mastermind behind the murders to be the town's mayor, who wanted to create a horror movie-themed amusement park and needed real-life inspiration.
