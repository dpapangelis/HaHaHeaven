Meet the Team!

Cascading Ideas Alliance

From the very start of the HaHaHeaven project, we decided that we wanted to produce a website that would showcase not only the skills acquired during this semester, but that would also be the fruit of team effort, rather than a cut and stitch combination of individual work.

Not only that, but we wanted to achieve the hardest requirement of this assignment; be creative. To that end we employed two means: our rampant imaginations and the permitted use of GenAI to create the content of our website.

As weeks progressed, we had many meetings. Ideas kept on coming and our code pages kept becoming longer. All this culminated in the creation of HaHaHeaven, which we are now in the proud position to present.

Working on this project has been not just fun, thanks to our amazing teamwork, and the nature of our selected subject, but a deeply enriching learning experience and an absolute joy!
